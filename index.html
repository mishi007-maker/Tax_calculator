Kenya Salary & Tax Calculator

This calculator helps employees and employers estimate salary deductions according to Kenyan tax regulations. It calculates NHIF, NSSF, NHDF, PAYE and Net Salary automatically using your basic salary and benefits.

What This Calculator Does:

This system calculates statutory deductions based on Kenyan tax regulations and gives an estimate of the employee's net salary.

How It Works:

Enter your basic salary and benefits. The system automatically calculates:

  • Gross Salary
  • NHIF Contributions
  • NSSF Deductions
  • NHDF Contributions
  • PAYE Tax
  • Net Salary

Why Use It?

It helps employees understand how salary deductions affect their earnings and allows employers to estimate payroll deductions quickly and accurately.
